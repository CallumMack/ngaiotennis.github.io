To make a booking or check availability use ngaio.skedda.com

The booking system is for members only

For details on how to join see the seniors and juniors sections.

Junior Members can book between 3pm to 6pm on work days only

They are still welcome to play at other times if the courts are not otherwise occupied.

If your kids are junior members and you are looking for a time outside of that window to play with your kids then you will have to take your chances a bit. Check the availability with the link above (do not book), keep in mind that senior members have priority in the evening and on weekends and may not book the courts before playing (because they may be happy to play with any other senior members that turn up). But when the courts have not been booked there is usually at least one court available.

If your kids like table tennis or foosball, perhaps come along to the end of club day on a Sunday (usually finishes around 5pm). The club rooms will be open until then and the chances are good that the courts will be free afterwards (unless the weather is particularly nice).
